To deploy javadoc:
	mv target/site/apidocs target/site/{version}
	scp -r target/site/{version} {username}@web.sf.net/home/project-web/xenqtt/apidocs/{version}

To deploy release files:
	mkdir target/{version}
	cp target/*.jar target/{version}/*.jar
	scp -r target/{version} {username}@frs.sf.net/home/frs/project/xenqtt
